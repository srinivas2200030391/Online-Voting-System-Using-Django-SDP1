ANDHRA PRADESH

Andhra Pradesh is a state in India's southern coastline region. With an area of 162,970 km2, it is India's sixth largest state. With 49,577,103 residents, it is the tenth most populated state.
Andhra Pradesh is a key rice-growing state as well as a big tobacco grower in India. The rivers of the state, including as the Godavari, Krishna, and Penneru, are vital to agriculture.
Andhra Pradesh is divided into three cultural regions: Uttaraandhra, Kostaandhra, and Rayalaseema.
